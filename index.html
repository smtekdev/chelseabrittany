The Artist

Hello, I'm Chelsea Brittany

What began as small custom orders from home has grown into a working studio built around one idea: art should be made by hand, for the person who asked for it.

"Every resin pour and every canvas is one of one — made for the person who asked for it, customizations always included."

Based in Washington, D.C., I split my time between resin casting and canvas — acrylic, abstract and watercolor — and I take on custom commissions at no extra charge.
